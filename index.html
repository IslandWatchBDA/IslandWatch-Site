FAQ

Clear answers. No fluff.

What is IslandWatch?

IslandWatch is a Bermuda‑built tracking and alerting service that helps protect your bike from theft, while also turning ride data into practical insights like a Safe Driving Score.

Is this only for motorcycles?

Bike theft is our launch focus, but the same platform supports other use cases such as new riders (16+), company vehicles, and select mobile assets.

Do you install the device?

Yes — installs are done in‑house so setup is consistent and supported from day one. After you join the waitlist, we’ll contact you to schedule.

What alerts will I receive?

You can receive alerts for things like movement/tamper security alerts, zone entry/exit (geofences), collisions and other unusual activity. During the pilot we tune alerts to reduce false alarms in Bermuda conditions.

How does the Safe Driving Score work?

At a high level, it summarizes riding patterns (like speeding events and harsh braking/acceleration) into an easy score and trends. It’s built to help riders improve over time — not overwhelm you with charts.

Can parents use this for new riders starting at 16?

Yes. The Safe Driving Score and ride history can help families coach safer habits early. We focus on practical insights and peace of mind.

What does the monthly plan include?

The launch plan includes all features — live tracking, alerts, geofences, and the Safe Driving Score. Device and install pricing is provided during onboarding.

Do you share my data with anyone?

Your data is used to provide the service. Any sharing (like a live‑location link) is initiated by you. Partner programs may come later, but nothing is shared without clear user consent.
